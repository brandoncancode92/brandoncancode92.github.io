My career as a Software Engineer

As far as I can remember, I have always admired the potential for technology and have been interested in learning more about it. In December of 2021 I decided to read a book teaching the basics of Python. I was fascinated at what could be done with just a few lines of code. I wanted to understand more, so I decided to take a private 12 week course from a Software Engineer.

My first project

Following several weeks of intensive study it was time to build my first project using Python! I created a NBA prediction app that would tell a user which of two teams was likely to win by pulling data from a API. It was a life changing experience, and with that experience, I decided to become a Software Engineer.
